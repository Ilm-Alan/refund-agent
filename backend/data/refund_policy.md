# Refund policy

Effective date: 2026-01-01. This policy governs all customer refund requests.
Support agents (human or automated) may not grant exceptions beyond what is
written here. Each rule below has a stable ID (R1-R8) that must be cited in
every refund decision.

## Decision precedence

Rules are evaluated in this order. The first blocking rule ends the evaluation:

1. R6 (fraud review freeze)
2. R2 (non-refundable categories)
3. R1 / R5 (refund window, including the VIP extension)
4. R4 (refund frequency limit)
5. R3 / R8 (condition, fees, and amount)

## R1. Refund window

Refunds apply to delivered items only, and must be requested within 30
calendar days of the delivery date. Requests outside this window are denied.
Orders that have not yet been delivered are handled through order
cancellation, not the refund process.

## R2. Non-refundable categories

The following are final and never refundable, regardless of the refund window:

- Items marked final sale at purchase
- Gift cards (physical or digital)
- Perishable goods (food, flowers, and other items with limited shelf life)
- Digital products and downloads once delivered

## R3. Condition and restocking

- Items reported defective, damaged in transit, or materially not as
  described: full refund of the item price.
- Electronics returned as "changed mind" that have been opened: refund minus
  a 15% restocking fee.
- All other eligible returns: full refund of the item price.

Customers state the item condition and reason during the request; claims may
be audited after the fact.

## R4. Refund frequency limit

A customer may receive at most 3 refunds in any rolling 365-day period.
Requests beyond the third are denied and referred to email support for manual
review.

## R5. VIP extended window

Customers with VIP status have the refund window in R1 extended from 30 to 60
calendar days, for items priced at 200.00 USD or less. Items above 200.00 USD
follow the standard 30-day window even for VIP customers. All other rules
apply to VIP customers unchanged.

## R6. Fraud review freeze

Accounts flagged for fraud review receive no automated refunds. Any refund
request on a flagged account must be escalated to a human specialist. The
agent must not disclose the flag itself; the customer is told the request
requires manual review.

## R7. Refund method and timing

Refunds are issued to the original payment method only, within 5-10 business
days of approval. Store credit substitutions are not offered.

## R8. Refund amounts

- A refund never exceeds the amount paid for the item.
- Shipping charges are refunded only when every item on the order is refunded
  for a defect or not-as-described reason; otherwise shipping is not refunded.
- Refunds are computed per item; an order containing a non-refundable item can
  still receive a refund for its eligible items.
